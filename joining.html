How to Join TSA

Joining Chamblee's TSA club is simple. You should consider joining TSA if you want to gain valuable leadership skills for use in today's workship, learn about technology, and meet a new and diverse cast of people.
Entry dues are $25, which should be given to the school's TSA chapter advisor (Ms. Gant in room 2132). After you join, you should also add yourself into our Remind group by texting @6chedd to 81010.
